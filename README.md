# 🏃‍♂️ TintFull: Interactive Night Running Wristband

> **Run out your own color with Tintfull.** Running at night without reflective gear can be dangerous and easily overlooked. To reduce the risks of night running and make fitness more engaging, we designed **TintFull**—an interactive night running wristband. 

This repository contains the **presentation website** designed to showcase the TintFull project, its features, and its companion application. 

## 💡 The Concept

TintFull goes beyond traditional safety gear. It not only provides visibility in the dark but also allows runners to compete remotely with friends. Even if you are miles apart, you can enjoy the thrill of running together. 

We chose a wristband design to completely free the runner's hands while allowing real-time measurement and tracking. Users can easily check their race time and results on the go without breaking their stride.

## ✨ Core Features

* **Night Safety Visibility:** Illuminates the runner in the dark, significantly reducing the risk of accidents.
* **Remote Racing:** Connect with friends anywhere in the world for real-time running competitions.
* **Hands-Free Tracking:** Effortlessly track race times and physical performance without holding a device.
* **Companion App Integration:** Syncs seamlessly via Bluetooth/Wi-Fi to our dedicated mobile app to collect match records and statistics.
* **Color Mixing System:** Choose your wristband's color via the app. After competing with a friend, your colors will "mix" to create a brand new color! 
* **Color Library:** Collect your newly mixed colors in your personal app library to use on your next run.

## 🛠️ Hardware & Origin

This project is built as an extended application of the **DSI5168** development board and was developed in association with the **ideas Hatch** hardware accelerator.
